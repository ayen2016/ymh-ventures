About Voktis

Voktis is a UK-based IT services company providing reliable technology solutions for modern businesses. It specialises in managed IT support, cybersecurity, cloud services and Microsoft upgrades. Originally known as Techienology Ltd, the company rebranded to Voktis Ltd in November 2022 to reflect its growth. Voktis focuses on delivering secure, efficient and scalable IT systems tailored to each client’s needs. With a customer-centric approach, the company helps organisations strengthen their IT infrastructure and operate with confidence.
